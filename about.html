About

Nina was born and raised in Dongguan, China. Nina is a Shih-Tzu and 'unknown' dog mix. Although she may have been born on April 1st, 2011, she is by no means a fool. Nina enjoys walks on sunny days and, most of all, treats. She devours all kinds-bones, steak bites, bone biscuits and even training treats (though she knows not a single trick). Nina also appreciates all kinds of human food, such as ham, cheese, bacon, and, at times, carrots. Nina despises cats and will bark relentlessly and spiritedly at cruel furballs. However, due to her growing age, her sight is not as it sharp as it once was and now struggles to see these feline menaces no matter how close they may be. Nina's sense of hearing is, too, not as strong as many moons ago and so, Nina has difficulty listening to her devoted caregivers she refers to as her loyal subjects and slaves. Of course this is all jokes as Nina is a fair and just ruler. Along with her human companions, Nina had a sister named Zuma who unfortuantely passed away when she was just 5 years of age. Nina is a versed and a well-travelled canine. She has been to a multitude of different realms including but not limited to Denmark, Greece, Germany and the Netherlands. Nina is remarkably intelligent so far as to understand the concept of time to calculate the exact hour the older male caregiver returns home whence he works all day (away from Nina). Nina does so in order to grip her paws on the treat he provides her with each time. In doing so, she understands that whenever a slave, sorry, caregiver steps foot into the garage, she is acquiring a biscuit, leading to many personal misunderstandings and consequently leading the human companions to feel pity and empathy for Nina.
